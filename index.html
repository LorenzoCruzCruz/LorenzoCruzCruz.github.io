African American and Africana Studies: The Impact of Colonialism on Afro-Latin Music

Lorenzo Cruz-Cruz

Welcome! This websites intended purpose is to show colonialism's impact on music pre, during, and post colonialism. As you navigate the website, you'll find important figures that shaped current afro-latin music, a timeline of events, important context, and other elememts. Collectively, those sections correctly and efficiently explain the timeline of how colonialism destroyed afro-latin music, and how people redefined that destruction to recover true cultural identity.
