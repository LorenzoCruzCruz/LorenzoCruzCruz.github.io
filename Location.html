Locations

Brazil

The Tupi, Guaraní, Tupinambá, Jê, Xavante, Kayapó, Yanomami, and Pataxo Tribes were found here.

Cuba

The Taíno, Ciboney, and Guanahatabey Tribes were found here.

Puerto Rico

The Taíno Tribe was the most dominant group here.

Colombia

The Muisca, Tairona, Quimbaya, Zenú, Guahibo, Sikuani, Kogi, Arhuaco, Wiwa, Damana, Pasto, and Pijao Tribes were found here.

Dominican Republic

The Taíno Tribe was the most dominant group here.
